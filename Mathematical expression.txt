
........Mathematical Expressions......

 * Linear Function: x
 
 * Quadratic Function: x**2

 * Cubic Function: x**3

Trigonometric Functions:-
 
 *Sine Function: np.sin(x)
 *Cosine Function: np.cos(x)
 *Tangent Function: np.tan(x)

Exponential and Logarithmic Functions:-

  *Exponential Function: np.exp(x)
  *Natural Logarithm: np.log(x)
  *Absolute Value Function:np.sqrt(x)

  *Polynomial Function:-x**3 - 2*x**2 + x - 1
  *Damped Oscillation:- np.sin(x) / x
